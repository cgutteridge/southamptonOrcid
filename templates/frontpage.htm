ORCID is a unique researcher ID identifier you keep throughout your life, wherever you are. ORCID ids look like a credit card number, eg. 0000-0001-9201-5987.

It is increasingly used by funders and publishers globally to identify publications and grants to people. Find out more about why ORCID can be useful. [TODO:link to our FAQ page]
